A hypercasual robot racer built in Unity. Send your droid down the course, scoop up nuts and
bolts along the way, and reach the finish to clear the level — then watch the score tally up
on a confetti-filled victory screen and roll on to the next, tougher run.

A compact C# hobby project — level progression with escalating difficulty, collectible
pickups and a per-level score tally, a robots bonus, and snappy win-screen juice (confetti
and on-screen flourishes), all wired up in the Unity engine and tuned for one-thumb mobile
play.
