A hypercasual boat runner built in Unity. Pick a jet ski from the garage, race it down a
winding water course, pass through multiplier gates to crank up the score, and plough
through clusters of props for a shower of physics-driven juice.

A compact C# hobby project — boat selection with upgradeable stats (speed, lives, coins),
course and obstacle spawning, swipe steering, multiplier-gate scoring, and collision
feedback (particles, confetti, and on-screen flourishes), all wired up in the Unity engine
and tuned for one-thumb mobile play.
