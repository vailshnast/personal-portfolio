An interactive negotiation-skills trainer built in Unity and shipped inside a corporate
Learning Management System (LMS). Instead of a slide deck, the learner steps into a 3D office
scenario and works a deal end to end — reading the other side, weighing offers, and making
scored choices that play out in real time.

The build teaches the core mechanics of principled negotiation through hands-on practice:
setting and holding a **Walk-Away Point**, recognising the **anchor** the other party opens
with, and steering toward the **Zone of Possible Agreement (ZOPA)** where a deal can actually
land. Decisions are gamified — a running **points** score rewards sound moves — and inline
true/false knowledge checks reinforce the theory the moment it's relevant, with immediate
correct/incorrect feedback rather than an end-of-course quiz.

The whole experience is touch-friendly ("tap anywhere to continue"), so it runs the same on a
laptop or a tablet — fitting neatly into an LMS course flow.
