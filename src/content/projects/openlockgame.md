A hypercasual tap-timing game built in Unity. Coloured pegs sweep around a rotating
padlock — tap at the right instant to lock each one into its slot, line them all up, and
pop the lock open. Clear the lock to advance, and every level cranks the speed and the peg
count a little higher.

A compact C# hobby project — the rotating-pin mechanic, hit-window timing and scoring,
level progression with escalating difficulty, and snappy juice (colour-shifting themes,
pop feedback, and on-screen flourishes), all wired up in the Unity engine and tuned for
one-thumb mobile play.
