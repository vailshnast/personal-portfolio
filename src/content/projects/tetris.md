A Tetris-inspired block-puzzle game built in Unity. Drag gem-block pieces onto the grid,
fill rows and columns to clear them, and chain clears together for escalating combo bonuses
against the clock.

A self-contained C# hobby project — gameplay grid, piece spawning and placement, line-clear
detection, scoring, and combo feedback (juice, particles, and on-screen flourishes), all
wired up in the Unity engine.
