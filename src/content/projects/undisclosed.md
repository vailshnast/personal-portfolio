_UNDISCLOSED is a 1-4 player co-op horror game where you play as an FBI agent investigating
paranormal events in the 1950s USA. Interact with witnesses, uncover hidden truths, and
prepare to face unexplained threats. Some things are better left undisclosed._

<div class="about-clip" data-about-clip="media/undisclosed/about/clip1"></div>

**BRIEFING**

Reports of strange occurrences are flooding in from _every corner of the country_.

_Your mission, agents, is simple:_ investigate, gather information, and neutralize any
threats. Keep everything under wraps—especially when dealing with witnesses. No information
will leave this operation.

**The safety of our nation is in your hands.** Stay sharp.

<div class="about-clip" data-about-clip="media/undisclosed/about/clip2"></div>

**IMMERSIVE INVESTIGATION EXPERIENCE**

- **Full immersion in the agent's role:** The minimalist interface keeps you focused on the
  investigation and puts you in the shoes of an FBI agent fully immersed in the case.
- **Witnesses:** Use a variety of methods to extract information from witnesses or
  suspects, including interrogation and threats. Some situations can't be resolved
  peacefully—be prepared for unpredictable reactions. And what if they're not human anymore?
- **On-site investigation:** Collect evidence, conduct searches, and break into homes to
  gather key information. Every action you take will either help you solve the case or simply
  survive…

<div class="about-clip" data-about-clip="media/undisclosed/about/clip3"></div>

**SECRET FILES: PARANORMAL THREATS**

- **Intriguing cases:** Each mission is a stand-alone, unique story inspired by iconic
  paranormal plots.
- **Unique mechanics:** Each threat is a unique challenge with mechanics that force you to
  adapt your tactics and approach to solving the problem.

<div class="about-clip" data-about-clip="media/undisclosed/about/clip4"></div>

**REPLAYABILITY FACTORS**

- **Randomized investigations:** Loot, building conditions, witnesses, and evidence are
  randomly generated for each mission, ensuring that no two cases feel the same.
- **Dynamic boss encounters:** Boss encounter strategies include unpredictable elements that
  require players to stay on their toes and adapt in real time.

<div class="about-clip" data-about-clip="media/undisclosed/about/clip5"></div>

**COOPERATIVE**

- **Designed for cooperative play:** The game's mechanics are built to encourage teamwork,
  with players taking on complementary roles to achieve common goals.
- **Immersive voice chat:** The in-game voice system takes into account obstacles and
  distance, providing realistic and engaging communication during missions.
